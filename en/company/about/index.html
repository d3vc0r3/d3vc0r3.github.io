Founded in 2012, DEVCORE believes that teaching real hacker mentalities and attack techniques is the only way to help enterprises improve their understanding of cybersecurity and protection. Today, DEVCORE applies even more comprehensive methods to greatly improve the cybersecurity and response capabilities of enterprises so they can focus on their core business.

DEVCORE is the first cybersecurity company to propose "Red Team Assessment" in Taiwan, composed of world-class white hackers and experts with hacker skills and mindset. In the spirit of challenging the limits and developing innovation, we focus on creating a safer network environment for the enterprise and world with our professionalism and passion for cybersecurity.

DEVCORE believes that outstanding cyber protection is essential to modern enterprises. It can strengthen your brand image and enhance consumer loyalty and trust. Consequently, identifying the fundamental issues in cybersecurity architecture is the first step to improve the security strategy for enterprise. DEVCORE's value and expertise lie in accurately pinpointing the source of problems to allow the efficient investment of enterprise resources into prioritized areas.

DEVCORE is familiar with the latest intel and attack methods, which means we can protect your products and services against constantly evolving cyberattacks; our record of publishing multiple vulnerabilities of renowned international products and services shows we are highly capable of identifying 0-day exploits ahead of hackers. DEVCORE's cybersecurity services will help you experience never-before-seen methods of infiltration, thereby assisting you in organizing your defense strategies and determining whether they can handle these types of attacks.
